Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Age, Income

The above chart shows the relationship between demographic factors and health risks for 50 states and the District of Columbia. Median age, household income, and poverty rates are plotted against the smoking, lack of healthcare, and obesity rates for each state or district. As may be expected, states or districts with lower income or higher poverty rates tend to show higher rates health risks.
